Hunger

How does Hunger affect literacy?

Many factors contribute to the literacy rates in Brazil, one major factor is hunger. Fristly, malnutrtion affects brain development especially during childhood when food is essential to the development of the brain. Hungry children are also more likely to drop out because of illness, fatigue, or the need to work. Parents who also faced hunger are more likely to repeat the cycle with their children, which highlights broader economic problems. While hunger is still prevalent in Brazil, the government has been working on it dilligently and has shown strong progress.
