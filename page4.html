Poverty: Literacy in Brazil

THE STRUGGLE BETWEEN POVERTY AND EDUCATION

One of the main issues affecting schooling in Brazil is poverty. Basic necessities like food, housing, and school supplies are out of reach for millions of families. Many kids drop out of school to support their families, particularly in rural and northern areas. The cycle of poverty is perpetuated by this lack of education, which makes it more difficult to obtain good jobs later in life. Giving every child an equal shot for a better future requires expanding access to high-quality education.
